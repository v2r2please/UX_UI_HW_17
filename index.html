Greetings! My name is Vero.

I am a UX/UI designer.

My goal as a designer is to make the world a bit friendlier through person centered research.

Tools in my toolbox includes Figma, Miro, GitHub, Visual Studio Code, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, Interaction Design, User Interface Design, and Usability Testing.

I also enjoy sci-fi, kung fu, drawing, and painting.

My UX Skills

User Research

Attention to details combined with an expanding toolkit supports my approach to user research. Proto-personas, personas, storyboarding, user journey maps, affinity diagrams, empathy maps, user interviews, user testing, 5 whys, and open ended questions are some strategies I find helpful.

User Interface Design

Familiarity with HIG and Material Design, style guides, and designing for accessibility are key elements in my design process. I am also proficient at freehand sketching, creating wireframes, prototypes, userflows, redlining, and heuristic analysis.

Front End Development

Being a solid team member is super important to me and so being code conversational is one of my priorities. I am familiar with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, GitHub, and Visual Studio Code. I make it a habit to keep learning new tools.

My Work

Federal Trade Commission website redesign

For this project, I worked independently to redesign the FTC's website for desktop and mobile. I performed user testing, redlining, heuristic evaluation, analyzed for accessibility, and developed wireframes, low and high fidelity prototypes while maintaining a sense of the original branding tone.

Play it Forward nonprofit redesign

With a team of fellow designers, I created a design update for pifmusic.org, a non-profit in the arts. My primary roles were in supporting design, creating a style guide, communicating with the client, wireframe creation, and presentation.
